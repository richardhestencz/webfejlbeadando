League of Legends Ranks

Iron

Iron is the lowest division in League of Legends. Players in this division are still learning the basics of the game.

Bronze

Bronze players have a better understanding of the game but may still struggle with certain aspects of gameplay.

Silver

Silver players have a good grasp of the game mechanics and are more skilled than Bronze players.

Gold

Gold players are considered above average and have a solid understanding of strategy and champion mechanics.

Platinum

Platinum players are highly skilled and demonstrate a deep understanding of the game, including advanced strategies.

Diamond

Diamond players are among the top percentage of players and possess excellent mechanical skills and game knowledge.

Emerald

Emerald is a prestigious rank, indicating exceptional skill and knowledge of the game.

Master

Master players are elite and possess exceptional skills, competing at a very high level of play.

Grandmaster

Grandmaster is an even more exclusive rank, representing the top echelon of League of Legends players.

Challenger

Challenger is the highest achievable rank, reserved for the absolute best players in the world.
