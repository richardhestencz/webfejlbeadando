Featured Champions

Yasuo

Yasuo, the Unforgiven, was once a proud swordsman from the prestigious Wind Slash style school. However, when he was falsely accused of murdering his elder brother, he was forced to flee into the night. Alone and pursued by both the Noxian invaders and the Ionian authorities, Yasuo became a skilled assassin, using his Wind Slash style to quickly and efficiently dispatch his enemies. Eventually, Yasuo found peace within himself and was ready to face his past. He sought out the elders of the Wind Slash style and confessed his mistake. The elders were surprised by his honesty and humility, and they allowed him to rejoin the Wind Slash style.

Volibear

Volibear Volibear, the Thunder's Roar, is a colossal ursine demigod who commands the thunder and lightning of Freljord. He is a force of nature, a living embodiment of the primal power of the frozen realm. Volibear was once a humble ursine shaman, revered by his people for his wisdom and connection to the spirit world. However, when a cataclysmic event shook Freljord to its core, Volibear's powers were awakened. He became a conduit for the raw elemental energy of the land, capable of summoning thunder and lightning at will.

Vi

Vi, the Piltover Enforcer, is a relentless and unstoppable force of justice. She is a skilled investigator and a formidable hand-to-hand combatant, using her cybernetic enhancements to dish out punishment to those who break the law. Before she was an enforcer, Vi was a street kid, growing up in the harsh underbelly of Piltover. She had to learn to fight to survive, using her brute strength and quick wit to protect herself and her friends. When Vi was teenager, she accidentally stumbled upon a secret underground fight club, where she was quickly recruited by a group of criminals who saw her potential. Vi quickly rose through the ranks of the criminal organization, becoming one of their most feared enforcers.

Senna

Senna, the Redeemer, is a spectral marksman who wields the power of the Black Mist to protect the innocent. She is a beacon of hope in a world shrouded in darkness, a reminder that even in the darkest of times, there is always a glimmer of light. Senna was once a young woman with a bright future ahead of her. She was a skilled marksman and a devout follower of the Moon Goddess, her archery serving as a symbol of hope and peace in the troubled land of Runeterra. However, Senna's life was shattered when she was captured by Thresh, a nightmarish wraith who preyed on the souls of the living. Thresh imprisoned Senna within the Black Mist, a realm of pure darkness where souls were trapped and tormented.

Lulu

Lulu, the Fae Sorceress, is a mischievous and unpredictable magical creature who delights in spreading joy and chaos. She is a master of whimsical spells, capable of conjuring up anything from playful illusions to mischievous pranks. Lulu is a native of the Glade of Peace, a magical realm hidden deep within the woods of Runeterra. She is a member of the Fae, a race of mischievous sprites who are known for their playful pranks and carefree attitude. Lulu is always up for an adventure, and she is always eager to spread her infectious joy to those around her. She is a friend to all creatures,big and small, and she sees the beauty in all things.
